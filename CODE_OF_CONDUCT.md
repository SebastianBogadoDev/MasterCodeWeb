# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the MasterCodeWeb project a respectful, welcoming, and professional experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful.
- Accepting constructive feedback.
- Helping other contributors.
- Communicating professionally.
- Respecting different opinions.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment.
- Offensive language.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing confidential information.
- Spam or abusive behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate corrective actions when necessary.
